Hi! I am currently a third-year M.S. student at Graphics & Geometric Computing Lab at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) , advised by Prof. Ligang Liu. I am fortunate to be able work with Prof. Bo Ren at Nankai University in my sophomore year, and Prof. Tao Du at Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences (IIIS), Tsinghua University from the summer of 2023.

I am applying Ph.D. for 2024 fall. You can find my CV here.

Reaserch Interest:
Currently, I am interested in differentiable simulation for solving inverse control / design problems, and AI + simulation, e.g. accelerating complex FEM and contact simulations with neural networks, leveraging large language models as data servers / world models for 3D content generations, etc.

Past Experiences:
I received my B.E. in CS with honor from USTC in 2021. During my undergraduate years, I was fortunate enough to be advised by Prof. Cheng Li on the reaserch topic of distributed training of deep neural networks in my third-year, and Prof. Pedro Lopes at the Human-Computer Integration lab the University of Chicago working on VR haptic rendering and intellectual medical wearable deivice in 2019 summer. I also did a summer industry internship at the infrastructure department of TikTok, Bytedance working on real-time physics simulation in 2020.
